How to run the app on a call
1
Before the call: Open the tool in a browser tab and keep it ready. It displays only published Forrester vendor names and scores — no internal Workvivo information is visible.
2
Walk the prospect through the leaderboard. In Forrester's default view, Workvivo is ranked a Leader at #3 — just 0.01 points from joint first place.
3
Select the relevant focus area — either HR & Communications or IT & Technical — to filter the leaderboard by the criteria most relevant to that buyer's priorities.
4
Ask the prospect: "Are there any other capabilities that are especially important to your organisation?" — then add those criteria to the view so the comparison reflects their specific evaluation criteria.
